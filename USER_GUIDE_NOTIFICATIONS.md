# **User Guide: Ntfy.sh Notifications**

This guide explains how to subscribe to notifications from your distributed AI knowledge mesh using the `ntfy.sh` app on your phone.

## **1. Install the `ntfy.sh` App**

*   **Android:** Install the `ntfy` app from the [Google Play Store](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=io.heckel.ntfy) or from [F-Droid](https://f-droid.org/en/packages/io.heckel.ntfy/).
*   **iOS:** Install the `ntfy` app from the [App Store](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/ntfy/id1625396347).

## **2. Subscribe to the Topic**

1.  Open the `ntfy` app on your phone.
2.  Tap the **+** icon to subscribe to a new topic.
3.  In the "Topic name" field, enter the following topic:
    ```
    omnibus-brain-notifications-v3
    ```
4.  Tap "Subscribe".

## **3. That's It!**

You will now receive notifications on your phone whenever a critical event occurs in your Semantic Brain system.
